- Instrumentation and Observational Techniques
- Interdisciplinary Research

I am actively seeking **Master's and Ph.D.** opportunities, with a strong commitment to continuous learning and skill development, especially in the **STEM** fields. I believe in constantly expanding my knowledge to keep pace with the latest advancements.
